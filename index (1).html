§02 — About the studio

I make slowly, in pieces, returning to the same questions in different rooms.

This studio is a quiet archive. Most of the work happens at the kitchen table, on long walks, between songs. It is paint and pixel and word and sound. It is about weather, memory, the small things that build a life.

I live with Neurofibromatosis Type 1 (NF1). It is part of the work, not all of it — there is a whole room here dedicated to learning, resources, and community for anyone navigating it too.

Other rooms in the studio.

Each space carries its own weather. Step into whichever room you're in the mood for — the writing is slow, the music is moody, the photography is quiet, and the NF1 corner is open and warm.
