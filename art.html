§02 — How buying works

A slow, honest exchange.

Each piece is one of one — original, signed, made by hand. There's no cart, no instant checkout. Just a small note and a real conversation about pricing, shipping, and care.

01

Send a note

Click inquire on any available piece. A small form opens — your name, email, and anything you want to say. Takes about a minute.

02

I reply, personally

Usually within a few days. You'll get pricing, shipping options to your country, and any details about the piece you want to know.

03

Pay & ship

Once we're aligned, payment via Venmo, PayPal, or another method that works for you. The piece is packed carefully and on its way.
